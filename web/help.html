Tremper Help

This application was created to assist people in sharing rides.
You begin by filling basic information about yourself. If you have done this before the application will take you automatically to choose whether you want to offer a ride (Tremper) or get a ride (Trempist)
Once you choose you are taken to a page where you can post a ride request\offer and view all the current ride requests\offers available. There is also the option of modifying your information.
